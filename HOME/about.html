ABOUT


Feeling fulfilled, productive, active, and motivated during a pandemic presents itself as quite the challenge, especially when our way of life has been completely altered. In todayâ€™s world, we are stuck in our own bubbles, unable to live and act without a care due to the risk of contracting Covid-19 and spreading it among our social circles. Even so, it is important that we have good outlets for our time and energy. Finding the right activities that are safe to our community and satisfying to our minds and bodies can feel like a daunting task. This is where we come in. Our team is pushing our community to pursue gardening within their homes, educating them on the ins and outs of a hobby that can be extremely fulfilling as well as beneficial to the individuals who participate in it. We want to encourage you to finally grow those basil leaves on your balcony, or to turn your sun porch into a mini jungle of tomatoes and bell peppers. We want to inspire community gardens on city blocks and backyard blooms. After all, gardening can be done anywhere there is light, water, space, and motivation.
