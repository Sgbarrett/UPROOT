Xeriscaping

Landscaping Without Water Waste

Seven Principles of Xeriscaping

Xeriscape, or dry-scape, is a method of landscaping that enhances watering efficiency and reduces the maintenance required to sustain your garden or landscape. This approach lets you conserve water and leads to a healthy and thriving landscape.

Xeriscaping is based on several water-wise landscape principles:
  • Plotting and Design
  • Soil Improvement
  • Practical Turf Area
  • Efficient Irrigation
  • Mulch
  • Low Water-use Plants
  • Appropriate Maintenance

Benefits

Xeriscaping has many benefits in areas where water may be sparse, including reducing water use by up to 75%. Installing irrigation systems like drips and soaker hoses gets water directly to the base of the plant and prevents the waste and evaporation levels of sprinkler systems. Grouping plants with similar needs and planting native plants also leads to more effective irrigation and lower maintenance requirements.
