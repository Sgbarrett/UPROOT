HOUSE PLANTS 101

How to keep your plants alive.

____________

Potting media

Every plant prefers a different type of potting media. From orchids to tomatoes this guide should give you a good idea as to what potting media is best for your plants.

____________

Water

Plants need water that goes without saying, but too much water can kill them. Follow this guide below to find out how to properly water your plants without killing them.

____________

Light

All plants need light in order to generate food and grow through photosynthesis. Too much light though can kill your plants and cause the leaves to burn. Having the proper amount of light will ensure your plants can grow to the best of their abilities.

____________

Fertilizer

Over time plants need to be fertilized to properly give them the right amount of nutrients in order to grow. Too much fertilizer can cause them to burn and eventually kill them. click the link below to learn how to properly fertilize your plants.

____________

Humidity

Humidity plays a big part in the happiness of your plants. Some plants prefer to be in an environment that has high humidity such as the many plants found in tropical environments. Find out more about humidity by clicking the link below.

____________
