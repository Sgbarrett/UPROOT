Hydroponics

Hydroponics is a fantastic way to grow lush vegetables and herbs in the comfort of your home. The roots sit in a body of fertalized water allowing the plants to have an optimal amount of nutrients. If you are limited on space this is the growing method for you. Click the link above to learn more.

About us

Hard working group of individuals looking to better the world through urban gardening. In a world where climate change is constantly becoming a reality it's important to make a difference where we can by educating the masses on how to do their part. We believe by helping people create their own gardens we can change the way people view sustainability and reduce our carbon footprint while helping others grow their own organic produce.
